Flexible containers

Pillow bag

Simple bag with top and bottom sealing. Common for snacks and dry products.

Gusseted bag

Has side gussets that expand its capacity. Used for coffee or grains.

Flat bottom bag

Flat base for greater stability. Ideal for premium foods.

Stickpack

Thin, elongated tube. Perfect for single portions like powders or liquids.

Sachet

Flat, small bag. Used for samples, condiments, or cosmetics.

Brick Bag

Rigid rectangular shape. Common for liquid or pasty foods like soups.

Flow bag

Wraps products with a single roll of film. Widely used in confectionery or bakery products.

Quattro seal bag

Sealed on all four edges. Provides high strength and good presentation.

Stand-up Pouch

Stands upright on its own. Ideal for snacks, granola, or liquid products.

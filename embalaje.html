Flexible Bags

We offer high-quality flexible bags for packaging, with options ranging from single layers to aluminum barrier films.

Pillowbag

Pillow-shaped bag sealed on the sides and ends. Economical and used for snacks and light products.

4-Sided-Sealed

Bag sealed on all sides, very secure. Used for food, cosmetics, or medical products.

Flat Top

Rectangular bag with flat base and top. Ideal for dry products like coffee, cereals, grains, powders, or snacks.

Clip Bag

Flexible bag closed with clip or tie. Resealable, perfect for fresh foods.

Angular

Bag with angled seal and firm shape. Ensures good presentation and stability.

Side-Sealed Bags

We offer bags with sealed edges, perfect for portioned products like single-serve dessert packs or mixed spices.

3-Side Sealed Bag

Sealed on 3 edges. Ideal for flat products, small portions, or sample packaging.

4-Side Sealed Bag

Sealed on all sides. Provides greater protection and security.

4-Side Base Bag

With bottom fold, more space. Ideal for liquids or bulky products.

Square Bottom Bag

We offer paper bags with square bottoms in various sizes, ideal for cereals and sugar, with options for heat sealing, viewing windows, and inner coating.

Block Bottom Bag

Square or rectangular base, very stable. Ideal for products that must stand upright.

Boxes

We offer boxes with bag-in-box system, providing greater protection and allowing customized inner bags and additional packages.

Bag-in-Box

Flexible bag inside a rigid box. Used for liquids like wine, juices, or oils.

Cans

We offer round cans with lids, ideal for small quantities, available in membrane, shaker, or combined versions, and in custom sizes.

Round Can

Metal container with airtight lid. Ideal for powders, food, or cosmetics.

Tray Technique

We offer trays in various sizes for optimal presentation on shelves, with options for multiple rows and automatic mixing of assorted products.

Tray Tech 1

Simple, low, open tray. Ideal for fruits, bakery, deli items, or display use.

Tray Tech 2

Tray with higher walls. Provides better protection and product organization.
